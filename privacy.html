Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

UnBeaten Desire ("we," "this site") respects your privacy. This page explains what information is collected when you visit unbeatendesire.com and how it's used.

Information we collect

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily subscribe to the weekly newsletter. Used solely to send you new posts. Never sold or shared with third parties.
  • Comments — any name and message you submit in the comments section is publicly visible on the post.
  • Reactions and quiz results — anonymous, aggregate interaction data (e.g. which emoji you clicked) is stored to understand what resonates with readers. Not linked to your identity.

Cookies and advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Data storage

Subscriber emails, comments, and reaction data are stored securely via Supabase. Reasonable technical measures are in place to protect this data, but no online system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time via the link in any email.
  • Request deletion of your comment or subscriber data by reaching out via the Contact page.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via the Contact page.

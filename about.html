About

UnBeaten Desire started in 2011 as a place to write down the things that felt too big to just think and let go of — love, heartbreak, faith, family, and the small, strange details of being a person trying to figure it out.

Fifteen years later, it's still that. Confessional essays, a little astrology, some letters that were never meant to be sent anywhere else, and stories that hopefully feel less like a diary and more like a conversation with a friend who happens to overthink everything the way you do.

New stories go up most weeks. If one of them makes you feel a little less alone in whatever you're carrying, that's the whole point.

— Shashank
